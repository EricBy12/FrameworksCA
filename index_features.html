Meditations

Choose from a number of recomended daily guided and non-guided meditations.
Design your personal guided or non-guided meditation session.
Choose a playlist or select form a series of soundscapes to go with your custom meditations.
Choose the duration of your session to suit your schedule.
Rate your experience to contribute to your personal Mood Graph.

Journaling

Create a daily journal to keep track of your thoughts and moods.
Add tags to your journal entries to better keep track of certain feelings during the day.
Sort entries by tags to help find certain entries in the journal.
Record entries with voice recoding for when you are in a hurry or don't want to type one out.
Give each day a rating to track your mood and also contribute to your personal Mood Graph.

Mood Tracking

The Mood Tracking feature combines the ratings from your Meditation and Journal entries to create a personalised graph to give you visual data on your thoughts, feelings and moods throughout the weeks and months.
This feature helps you to track improvemnets in your mental health, but can also highlight things and situations you should try to avoid.

Healthy Eating

A healthy body is a helathy mind.
Clear Path provides you with a number of recomended recipies for breakfast, lunch and dinner.
Search for specific meals by using the ingredient filter so you can single out allergens or find vegan friendly meals.
Each recipie has a list of ingredients, simple and easy to follow instructions.
There is also infomation on how long the meal will take to cook, how many people it will serve and how many calories are in the dish.
